About Me

I am Michael, a graduand from Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at President Ramon Magsaysay State University - Main Campus. I have a strong background in web development, software engineering and machine learning, having spent the past 2 summers interning at PRMSU Botolan. ICT office and IPhitech IT and Digital Solutions, along with more than 2 years of freelancing experience.

Additionally, in our thesis study, my team and I worked on innovating a machine learning algorithm with the aim of bringing positive and transformative change in precision agriculture that would benefit the farmers. Throughout our study, I lead both the development of our machine learning model and mobile application, an effort that earned us the Best Thesis Award. As cliché as it may sound, I'm very passionate about using technology to create solutions that bring positive changes to people's lives.

Lastly, I dream of having a career like Guido van Rossum, the former Benevolent Dictator for Life of Python. I admire his work and influence on the programming community. And more importantly, he has a sick Python license plate.
